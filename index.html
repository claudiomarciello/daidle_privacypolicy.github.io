Privacy Policy

Daidle was built with privacy in mind.
Your drawings are stored locally on your device. You are the only person who can access this data. We do not track you, sell personal data or embed ad networks in the app.
Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy to learn more about how your data is processed.

Consent

By using Daidle, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.


Collected Data

Daidle is designed with your privacy in mind. Therefore, we do not collect, store, or have access to any personal data or information stored in the app. Your data is stored locally on your device only, except when you choose to upload drawings to your Photos app. This action does not transmit the image or any related data to our servers or third parties.


Third-Party Analytics

Our app does not include any third-party analytics services. We do not collect, store, or process any user data for analytical purposes.


Third-Party Advertising

Our app does not include any third-party advertising. Consequently, there are no ad networks involved, and no data is collected or shared for advertising purposes.


Data Sharing with Third Parties

We do not share any user or device data with third parties. As our app does not collect any data, there is no information to store or share with external entities.


Additional Data Collection

Our app does not collect any user or device data for any purposes beyond the normal functioning of the app. We prioritize user privacy and ensure that no personal or device information is collected, processed, or shared.


Permissions

Our app requires permission to access your Photos for the sole purpose of uploading images. This permission is strictly used to enable you to upload images to the app. We do not collect, store, or share these images or any related data with any third parties.



How We Use Your Information

Since Daidle does not collect, store, or access any user data, there are no means or purposes for which we could use personal information.
All data managed by the app is exclusively controlled and accessed by the user, with no third-party access facilitated by Daidle.


Future Change

The privacy policy may be updated in the future. Visit this page regularly to be aware of the policy you are agreeing to by using our services.
